Lamb Shank

Description

Lamb shank is a cut of meat from the lower leg of a lamb, prized for its rich flavor and succulent texture when slow-cooked.

The cut is naturally tough due to connective tissue, but this breaks down during long, slow cooking methods

like braising,resulting in a tender, melt-in-your-mouth result.

The bone adds flavor and richness, making it a popular choice for hearty, comforting meals.

Ingredients

  • 4 lamb shanks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ⅓ cup saba
  • ½ teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
